Decorating with plants is simple

To create the most interesting display, use a mixture of plant sizes
and leaf shapes. A large plant like Pippa the peace lily, mixed with
something sculptual like Susie the snake plant and as soft fern like
Bertie gives an attractive range of textures. Hanging plants, like
Rapunzel the golden pathes, add interest at different heights. Don't
forget your pots. Matching pots will look clean and elegant, while a
mix of styles and patterns looks electric and fun.
